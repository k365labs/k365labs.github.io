About Us

K365labs is an IT consultancy specializing in software quality engineering through DevOps and Business Intelligence.

We rely on Microsoft SQL Server Business Intelligence and Microsoft Visual Studio DevOps as well as other Business Intelligence and DevOps products in order to assist customers adopt DevOps and Business Intelligence.

We provide consulting services for a wide variety of DevOps and Business Intelligence products to small business and enterprise companies in industries such as Manufacturing, Research and Development, Education, and Utility.

K365labs delivers distinct advantages to our clients in delivering DevOps and business intelligence solutions. We are recognized across industries as one of the pioneers in DevOps and business intelligence, and our portfolio of successful industry services and solutions are testaments to our capabilities.
